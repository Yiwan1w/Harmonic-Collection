All moments are especially precious

At the Seaside

It's enjoyable to watch the sun rise little by little from the sea level and feel the passage of time.

Feeling the sea breeze blowing, enjoying the colors of the sunset, and tasting fresh seafood are the best things to escape from the hustle of the city life.

Into the Desert

Finally, I went the Sahara in the story, watching the endless desert, sitting outside the tent at night and counting the stars, experiencing camels, and waiting for the sunrise in the desert.

What's more, I got rather close to cactus in various heights and shapes in Pheonix.

Traveling through the city

I've been to many cities. Apart from the skyscrapers, heavy traffic and hustling people, I am happy to explore the inner culture of each city and the unique scenery created by this culture.

The sense of distance brought by a modern city will give me the freedom of being an outsider.

Go into the Mountains

The scenery on land always surprises me, from rolling mountains to raging waterfalls. The wonders of the outback are nature’s gifts to us.

These scenery always heal me from the fatigue caused by busyness and make my whole person look brand new.

Disney World

I'm a huge Disney fan. Walking into Disney makes me feel like I'm still a kid. I always have fun at Disney. The immersive experience brought by Disney can help me heal and enjoy happiness at the same time.

Among the many Disney characters, Winnie the Pooh is my favorite.

Special Trips

The most impressive trip for me was a teaching mission in Yunnan in 2019. This not only allowed me to experience the scenery of Yunnan, but also made me feel the sense of accomplishment as an educator and the gap between urban and rural areas.
