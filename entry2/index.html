Landed in Miami

Sea breeze, sunset, graffiti walls, colorful buildings, fashionable neighborhoods

The trip to Miami is a trip that comes and goes. On a chilly January day in New York, coastal cities are the first choice to escape the cold. Walking on the road in Miami, I was deeply attracted by this city. Miami is modern but not busy, fashionable and inclusive, and quiet.

Miami on Photographic Film

In Vizcaya Museum&Garden

Miami is very photogenic. Rich colors and lots of sunlight and vintage architecture are the perfect backdrop. Vizcaya Garden is a place that I like very much. The European-style buildings and huge gardens made me feel like I was in the last century. The texture of film makes these photos my favorite set of travel photos.
