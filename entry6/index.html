My starting with Disney

The first time I went to Disneyland was when I was seven years old, at Hong Kong Disneyland. There was no Disneyland in Shanghai at that time. But when I was young, I didn’t have a particular love for Disney. It was when I was in junior high school that Shanghai had its own Disneyland. In order to escape my busy studies, I began to go there frequently to find happiness. I gradually fell in love with the various cute characters and the immersive design of decoration in the park.

My exploration progress

Up to now, there are six Disney parks in the world, namely Shanghai Disney Resort, Tokyo Disney Resort, Hongkong Disneyland, Disneyland Paris, Disneyland Resort in California, and Walt Disney World Resort in Orlando.

I have only been to Disney in Shanghai, Hong Kong and Orlando, and there are three more waiting for me to explore.

I look forward to going to the rest of Disney in few years and meeting with Pooh all over the world!

"I can’t count how many times I’ve been to Shanghai Disney. It is no exaggeration to say that I can memorize the map of Shanghai Disney. It is also the newest Disneyland in the world."

Explore More Nearby

Compared with the other two Disneys I have been to, what I like most about Shanghai Disney is the combination of shopping and food outside of the park. Going out from the side gate of the park is Disney Town, which has many restaurants and brand stores, like Pandora, Nike, Swatch, etc. When I feel hungry, I can go to the Cheesefactory or hot pot restaurant for a hearty meal instead of the boring food in the park.
