Breaking Into the Secret Garden

In My Dreams

First Chapter -- Marrakech

This is Jardin Majorelle, my favorite garden ever. It is a one-hectare botanical and artist's landscape garden in Marrakech, Morocco. The Yves Saint Laurent Museum is also located in it. This garden embodies my idea of a secret garden. Imagine. The highly saturated royal blue, the rich variety of rare tropical plants, and the fountain with its own dreamcore atmosphere are all the reasons why I fell in love with this place.

Welcome to Sahara

I rode a camel, saw the starry sky, watched the sunrise in the desert, slept in a tent, and felt the campfire.

The yearning for Sahara reached its peak after reading Sanmao's "The Story of Sahara". I am very happy to be able to walk into the Sahara during the summer vacation of 2019 and experience the uninhibited freedom and romance of Sanmao.
