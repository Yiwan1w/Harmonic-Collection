One of the great charms of Miami is the fusion of cultures, which can be vividly demonstrated in the GASTRONOMY.

Miami is a hub with immigrants from all over the world and various cultures. The largest number of them are from Latin America and the Caribbean. Therefore, here, I found and tried a lot of different and new kinds of cuisine, like Cuban, Peruvian, Greek, typical Americian brunch, and some fresh seafood that I have never had before. The restuarants here are just like the city vibe, always bringing welcome and enthusiasm.
