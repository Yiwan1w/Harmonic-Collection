My brief journal

Yunnan is my dream retirement residence. The beautiful natural scenery, slow pace of life, various delicious foods, and longer sunshine hours all attract me.

I prefer the authentic environment here without the noise caused by too much machinery. I prefer the sunset here, without the shadow of high-rise buildings.
