Christmas in Disney

My First Christmas in US, in my favourite place.

The Christmas atmosphere at Disney is very strong. There are various Christmas trees and the dolls also wear Santa hats. What surprised me the most was the fake snow on Christmas Eve at Disney Spring. Night, Christmas trees and falling snow constitute my ideal Christmas scene. I spent Christmas this year with my best friend. This is our first year accompanying each other in a foreign land.

One of the things I particularly love about Disney is the detail. From all the decorations, to the food, to the dolls, to the music, it 100% immerses me in Christmas. At the same time, the warm weather in Orlando made this Christmas the most ritualistic and joyful Christmas ever.
