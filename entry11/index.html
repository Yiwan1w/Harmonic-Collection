Starting of this Journey

QDHS@Laoying Middle School

This is a traditional Christmas project at QDHS. Under the guidance of teachers, our high school has designed a complete two-week course with a total of 110 class hours. We went to Laoying Middle School in Baoshan, Yunnan to teach in person and went out to play with them. It has been an amazing journey for me, both as an educator and as an educated person.

Arrive in Baoshan

It took us about half a day to fly from Shanghai to Kunming, and then transfer to Baoshan, almost crossing half of mainland China.

This is the day before we start this project. We come to the school to understand the basic situation of the school. Here is a group photo of all our QDers involved in this project. For the first time that day, I saw my students, their smiling faces flushed from the sun, and their bright eyes revealing curiosity, doubt, and shyness.

Classtime

When people who wear school uniforms become teachers

As the group leader, I designed and completed Frisbee class with my group members. We needed to think ahead about the Frisbee we would need, the constraints of the venue, and the safety issues students might face. The students here are very enthusiastic and give us enough responses in class. I feel that this is the happiest moment as an educator.

In our spare time, we conducted home visits to some students in the class and felt helpless about the lack of parents in the family. Behind the warm companionship between grandparents and grandchildren is the lack of parental companionship for left-behind children in remote areas. I really like this home visit photo, the smiles on their faces are really warm.
